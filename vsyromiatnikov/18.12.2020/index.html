Honda NSX

The Honda NSX, marketed in North America as the Acura NSX, is a two-seat, mid-engine coupe sports car manufactured by Honda.

The NSX was designed by a team led by Chief Designer Masahito Nakano and Executive Chief Engineer Shigeru Uehara. It benefited from advanced aerodynamics and inspired by an F-16 fighter jet cockpit[5] and input from the late Formula One World Champion, Ayrton Senna, during the final development stages.
